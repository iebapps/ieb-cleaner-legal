Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Thank you for using IEB Cleaner. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This policy explains how we handle data inside the app.

1. No Personal Data Leaves Your Device

IEB Cleaner processes all photos, videos, storage information, and duplicates 100% locally on your device. No media files are uploaded, transmitted, or stored on any server.

2. Permissions We Request

  • Photos & Videos – required to identify large, duplicate, blurry, or old media files.
  • Storage Access – required to delete selected files after your confirmation.

3. Crash & Analytics

IEB Cleaner does not use analytics or Crashlytics at this time.

4. Advertising

Non-PRO users may see ads served through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect anonymous device identifiers in accordance with their own privacy policy.

5. PRO Subscription

If you purchase PRO, the purchase is stored locally and verified by the App Store / Play Store. No personal payment information is ever accessible to us.
